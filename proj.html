About Our AI Model

NeuroScan AI is powered by cutting-edge deep learning technology trained on thousands of brain MRI scans.

Model Architecture

EfficientNet-B2 Backbone

Our model uses EfficientNet-B2 architecture optimized for medical image analysis with 96.5% validation accuracy.

Training Dataset

Trained on 3,264 brain MRI scans from multiple hospitals, including glioma, meningioma, pituitary tumors, and healthy scans.

Performance Metrics

Accuracy: 96.5% | Sensitivity: 95.8% | Specificity: 97.1%

Tumor Classification

1 Glioma

Gliomas arise from glial cells and are the most common primary brain tumors. Our model detects them with 96.2% accuracy.

2 Meningioma

Meningiomas grow from the meninges and are usually benign. Detection accuracy of 97.1%.

3 Pituitary

Pituitary tumors develop in the pituitary gland. Our model identifies them with 95.9% accuracy.
